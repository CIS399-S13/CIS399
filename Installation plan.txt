Installation plan:

boot from USB-drive.

Step 1: choose "Install Ubuntu"
Step 2: make sure there is enough space for the system. (5.4G)
Step 3: Choose "Erase disk and install ubuntu", click "Install Now"
Step 4: Type-in the city.
Step 5: Choose keyboard layout(English(US))
Step 6: type-in user information, click "continue".
Step 7: wait for installation finish.
Step 8: When the installation finish, click "Restart Now"
